Subtractive Logic

Cut a hole in the market's surface and look at the machine underneath.

The method is subtractive: strip out what's already known — the factors, the beta, the market — and study what's left. Three linked instruments map the whole mechanism, from a fact on a company's books to a price, built on real ownership, filings and factor data and on cited research. A private, ongoing research project — a way of thinking about how markets work, not investment advice.
